In the year 2034...
Ken Castle invents self-replicating "nanites" that replace brain tissue and allow humans to control other humans' actions. After creating Society, a life sim, using this technology, Castle then creates the third-person shooter game Slayers. These two games make Castle the richest man in the world. While Society sees players control live actors, Slayers has them control death-row prisoners using real weapons in specified zones. One such Slayer is John "Kable" Tillman, having survived a record 27 matches. With 30 wins, Kable will walk free. He has other plans...
